About Us

LifeFlow is a comprehensive blood donation management system dedicated to connecting donors with those in need. Our mission is to ensure that no one suffers due to blood shortage.

We work with hospitals, blood banks, and donors to create a seamless ecosystem that makes blood donation accessible, efficient, and rewarding.

Founded in 2020, LifeFlow has facilitated over 50,000 donations and helped save more than 150,000 lives. Our network includes 200+ hospitals and blood banks across the country, making it easier for donors to find donation centers near them.

Eligibility Criteria

Age & Weight

  • • Must be at least 18 years old
  • • Weight at least 110 lbs (50 kg)
  • • No upper age limit for regular donors

Health Requirements

  • • Generally good health
  • • No fever or illness on donation day
  • • Adequate hemoglobin levels
  • • Normal blood pressure and pulse

Waiting Periods

  • • 56 days between whole blood donations
  • • 112 days after double red cell donation
  • • 7 days after plasma donation
  • • Varies after certain medications or vaccinations

Frequently Asked Questions

Generally, you can donate blood if you:

  • Are at least 18 years old
  • Weigh at least 110 lbs (50 kg)
  • Are in good health
  • Meet all eligibility requirements

The frequency depends on the type of donation:

  • Whole blood: Every 56 days
  • Platelets: Every 7 days (up to 24 times per year)
  • Plasma: Every 28 days
  • Double red cells: Every 112 days

To prepare for blood donation:

  • Get enough sleep the night before
  • Eat a healthy meal within 3 hours of donation
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Bring a valid ID and list of medications
  • Wear comfortable clothing with sleeves that roll up easily

The entire process takes about one hour:

  • Registration: 10-15 minutes
  • Health screening: 10-15 minutes
  • Actual donation: 8-10 minutes
  • Recovery with refreshments: 10-15 minutes
